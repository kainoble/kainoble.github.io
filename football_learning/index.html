Overview of Interactive Training

Interactive training in football involves using technology and innovative methods to simulate game-like scenarios, improve decision-making, and enhance player skills. It integrates virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), and interactive simulations to create realistic training environments.

Benefits of Interactive Training

Interactive training offers several advantages:

  • Enhanced engagement and motivation among players.
  • Real-time feedback and performance analysis.
  • Simulation of match scenarios to develop tactical awareness.
  • Customizable training sessions based on individual player needs.
